Iphone Tip





Today wish “The pleasantest things in the world are pleasant thoughts, and the great art of life is to have as many of them as possible.” Montaigne Why fill your head with gloom and doom? If your thoughts are dark and negative, it’s hard to see the good things in your life—people who care about you, opportunities for learning and fun, a future as bright as you want it to be. Just for today, try banishing all sad, self-defeating thoughts from your mind. See how you feel at the end of the day. You might write about this experience in your journal—and pledge to do it again tomorrow. TODAY I’ll think pleasant thoughts.
